AgriLink Africa — Digital Market for Farmers & Youth

Core Concept

AgriLink Africa is a blockchain-powered agribusiness platform designed to connect smallholder farmers directly with buyers such as supermarkets, schools, and restaurants. The platform improves income for farmers, creates jobs for youth as digital agents and coordinators, and generates revenue through transaction fees and premium data services.

Features

  • Farmer Access: Youth agents help farmers register and showcase produce.
  • Marketplace: Buyers place orders directly from farmers.
  • Smart Contracts: Secure, transparent payments via blockchain.
  • Analytics Dashboard: Crop demand and pricing trends for better decisions.
  • Logistics Coordination: Youth organize transportation from farms to buyers.

Benefits

  • Community Impact: Farmers receive fair prices and reliable buyers.
  • Youth Employment: Jobs created in logistics, tech support, and data analysis.
  • Revenue Generation: Transaction fees and premium analytics services.
  • Sustainability: Contributes to food security and child nutrition programs.
